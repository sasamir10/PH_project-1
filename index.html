Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before you register

What's included in my ticket? +

Your ticket includes access to all keynote talks, breakout sessions, hands-on workshops, sponsor demos, networking areas, meals, coffee breaks, and the DevConf 2026 evening reception.

Is there a refund or cancellation policy? +

Yes. Tickets are refundable up to 30 days before the event, minus processing fees. After that date, you may transfer your ticket to another attendee at no additional cost.

Do you offer group or team discounts? +

Yes. Teams registering five or more attendees are eligible for discounted group pricing. Contact our registration team and we will help arrange the best option for your group.

Is DevConf 2026 in-person only, or is there a virtual option? +

DevConf 2026 is primarily an in-person event, but select keynote sessions and featured talks will be available through a virtual pass for remote attendees.

Will sessions be recorded? +

Most keynote and breakout sessions will be recorded and shared with registered attendees after the conference. Some workshops may not be recorded due to their interactive format.

What's your Code of Conduct? +

DevConf 2026 is committed to a welcoming, inclusive, and respectful environment for everyone. All attendees, speakers, sponsors, and staff are expected to follow our Code of Conduct throughout the event.
